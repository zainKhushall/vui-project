Welcome to the Study on Context Preservation

This study aims to evaluate the context preservation capabilities of the system.

Context Preservation refers to the system's ability to remember the ongoing conversation and refer back to previous queries or responses when needed.

System Features:

  • Speech Recognition: Convert your speech to text for processing.
  • Text-to-Speech Output: Amazon Polly provides natural-sounding voice responses.
  • Weather Updates: Get real-time weather info by providing a city name.
  • Simple Web Search: The system can search the internet (accuracy not guaranteed).
  • Standard Conversation: Engage in normal, everyday dialogue.
  • Event Recorder: Schedule and store events like meetings with date and time.

Instructions:

After interacting with the system, you will complete a short questionnaire and provide feedback regarding the system's context preservation abilities.
